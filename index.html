About Us

We are Ready to Empower Futures Through Education.

A group of education and social enthusiasts initiated a learning center in Amagachi village, Bihar, addressing the region's educational and developmental gaps. Led by Vijay Paswan, the center, established in October 2022 with limited resources, currently supports 50-60 kids without formal school access. The team comprises volunteers juggling full-time jobs to sustain both their families and this meaningful initiative!

Our Aims are:-

Bridging educational gaps

Ensuring Equality

Provide diverse learning opportunities

Alleviating Poverty

Empowering Rural Communities

Eradacting Intoxication

Build skills and confidence of youth

Economic Growth by creating job opportunities

16 Sept 2023

Today, we organized our first weekly class at the Aatmvishwas Foundation Centre, marking the beginning of our new tradition of hosting unique activity sessions with the children every Saturday.
During today's session, we focused on personal hygiene by checking the children's nails. Those with overgrown nails had them trimmed, and we also conducted checks on their teeth and clothing. Moving forward, this activity will take place every Saturday to instill a sense of cleanliness and neatness among the children.

28 Sept 2023

Two volunteers joined the 'Atmasvishwas Foundation' on January 9th for a month-long stint, during which they made significant contributions and fostered mutual learning and growth. Both Ayush and Harsh diligently carried out their responsibilities at the 'Atmasvishwas Foundation', excelling in their project work and establishing strong bonds with the children and their families. To bid them farewell, they were presented with a towel and a book by the foundation's representatives. On behalf of the 'Aatmvishwas Foundation', I had the pleasure of awarding them with a one-month work experience certificate, acknowledging their dedication and commitment. Ayush and Harsh expressed their intent to remain engaged with the Aatmvishwas Foundation in the future and continue supporting its endeavors. The day concluded with heartfelt farewells, capturing memories with the children through photographs, and sharing sweet treats. Thank you for your valuable contributions.
