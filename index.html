About Us

We are Ready to Empower Futures Through Education.

A group of education and social enthusiasts initiated a learning center in Amagachi village, Bihar, addressing the region's educational and developmental gaps. Led by Vijay Paswan, the center, established in October 2022 with limited resources, currently supports 50-60 kids without formal school access. The team comprises volunteers juggling full-time jobs to sustain both their families and this meaningful initiative!

Our Aims are:-

Bridging educational gaps

Ensuring Equality

Provide diverse learning opportunities

Alleviating Poverty

Empowering Rural Communities

Eradacting Intoxication

Build skills and confidence of youth

Economic Growth by creating job opportunities

Prioritize and maintain personal hygiene for a healthier, happier life.

Today, we organized our first weekly class at the Aatmvishwas Foundation Centre, marking the beginning of our new tradition of hosting unique activity sessions with the children every Saturday. During today's session, we focused on personal hygiene by checking the children's nails. Those with overgrown nails had them trimmed, and we also conducted checks on their teeth and clothing. Moving forward, this activity will take place every Saturday to instill a sense of cleanliness and neatness among the children.

Learning from Online Classes conducted by team members and volunteers

Today, her mission is to empower children with the gift of language through an online English class focused on action words. Annu logs into Google Meet and share meeting link with Vijay Ji, greeted by eager faces of young learners of our center. With enthusiasm in her voice, she starts the class, engaging the children with interactive activities and lively discussions. Using creative teaching methods, Annu introduces 100 action words like "run," "jump," and "dance." She encourages the children to practice using these words in sentences, fostering their confidence and language skills. Despite the challenges of virtual learning, Annu's passion shines through. She patiently guides each child, providing individual attention and support, ensuring that no one is left behind, also gave take to preapre chart and hang on Aatmvishwas Center's wall . As the class comes to an end, Annu reflects on the impact of her work. Through education, she is breaking barriers and opening doors of opportunity for these children. She knows that empowering them with language skills today will transform their lives tomorrow. With a sense of fulfillment, Annu looks forward to the next class, knowing that she is making a meaningful difference, one English lesson at a time.
