Our Story

We are Ready to Empower Futures Through Education.

We, a group of education and social enthusiasts, came together to start a learning center in Amagachi village of Arariya - 854332 district in Bihar, which area is highly deprived of education, health, and development. Vijay Paswan, who belongs to Amagachi, started the learning center in October 2022 with limited resourse and strongest desire to serve community!

Currently, our initiative helps around 50-60 kids, many of whom do not have formal school access. It is in early stage right now registeration process is in progress.

All the team members who are working for this initiative are volunteers but also working full-time job to support themselves and their families. It takes a pipeline of organisations to let problem solvers live up to their fullest potential.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Empowering rural communities through education, guidance, and community engagement to foster lifelong learning and opportunities for socio-economic advancement.

Bridging Educational Gaps

We provide equitable access to quality education for all individuals, regardless of their background, to ensure everyone has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Empowering Rural Communities

We spread general awareness among villagers involves educating and informing them about their rights, resources, and opportunities for sustainable development.

Diverse Learning Opportunities

We offer educational experiences that cater different learning styles, interests, & needs, ensuring that all individuals can engage in meaningful & effective learning.

Skills and Confidence of Youth

We build skills and confidence in youth by connecting them with reputable NGO partners that provide training and employment opportunities.

Vision

Making sure every rural child gets good education, helping youth learn skills for good jobs, and working together to build better and fairer communities.

Alleviating Poverty

We works towards alleviating poverty by providing job placement assistance, empowering individuals to secure sustainable livelihoods & break the cycle of poverty.

Eradicating Intoxication

We involves educating youth and childern about alcohol or drugs harmful effects and implementing programs to prevent them from engaging in such behavior.

Ensuring Gender Equality

We promote equal rights, opportunities, and treatment for all genders, addressing discrimination and stereotypes to create a more inclusive society.

Character Building

Character building involves instilling positive values, ethics, and skills in individuals to help them develop strong moral principles and make responsible decisions in life.
