Personal Hygiene

Today, we organized our first weekly class at the Aatmvishwas Foundation Centre, marking the beginning of our new tradition of hosting unique activity sessions with the children every Saturday. During today's session, we focused on personal hygiene by checking the children's nails. Those with overgrown nails had them trimmed, and we also conducted checks on their teeth and clothing. Moving forward, this activity will take place every Saturday to instill a sense of cleanliness and neatness among the children.

Learning Action Words

Today, her mission is to empower children with the gift of language through an online English class focused on action words. Annu logs into Google Meet and share meeting link with Vijay Ji, greeted by eager faces of young learners of our center. With enthusiasm in her voice, she starts the class, engaging the children with interactive activities and lively discussions. Using creative teaching methods, Annu introduces 100 action words like "run," "jump," and "dance." She encourages the children to practice using these words in sentences, fostering their confidence and language skills.
